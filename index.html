National Students' Space Challenge

Going by the name of Space Technology Students' Society (spAts), this is the official student body under the Kalpana Chawla Space Technology Cell (KCSTC), which is the contact point of Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) at IIT Kharagpur. This student group has conceived and bred the first and the only fest in India that is solely dedicated to Space Technology - National Students' Space Challenge (NSSC).

National Students' Space Challenge

It organises the annual Space Technology and Astronomy themed fest, National Students' Space Challenge[1] (NSSC) every year 2011. spAts has been constantly instrumental in organizing space-themed talks, discussions, sky gazing sessions, lectures and presentations within IIT Kharagpur.
